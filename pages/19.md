```sql student_performance
SELECT 
    s.first_name, 
    s.last_name, 
    a.semester, 
    a.final_grade, 
    a.gpa_points, 
    a.attendance_rate 
FROM database_891377033216.academic_performance a 
JOIN database_891377033216.students s ON a.student_id = s.student_id;
```
