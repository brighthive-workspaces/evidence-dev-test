```sql students_attendance
SELECT
    student_name,
    attendance_rate
FROM
    attendance_records
ORDER BY
    attendance_rate DESC;
```

```jsx
<BarChart
    data={students_attendance}
    x=student_name
    y=attendance_rate
    yFmt=pct
    labels=true
    height=400
    colorPalette={["#007bff"]}
/>
