### SQL Query
```sql gpa_by_major
SELECT
    major,
    SUM(CAST(gpa_points AS FLOAT)) / CAST(COUNT(*) AS FLOAT) AS average_gpa
FROM brighthive_dev_redshift.academic_performance
GROUP BY major
ORDER BY average_gpa DESC;
```
