```sql students_attendance
SELECT
    student_name,
    attendance_rate
FROM
    student_attendance
ORDER BY
    student_name
```

```jsx
<BarChart
    data={students_attendance}
    x=student_name
    y=attendance_rate
    labels=true
    colorPalette={["#4e79a7"]}
    height=400
    xLabel="Student Name"
    yLabel="Attendance Rate (%)"
/>
```
