### **SQL Query**
```sql student_grade_distribution
SELECT
    final_grade,
    COUNT(*) as student_count
FROM brighthive_dev_redshift.academic_performance_1738358525184
GROUP BY final_grade
ORDER BY student_count DESC;
```
