#### **SQL Query: Student Attendance**
```sql student_attendance
SELECT
    student_name,
    attendance_rate
FROM
    student_attendance_data
ORDER BY
    attendance_rate DESC
```

#### **Markdown for Bar Chart**
```jsx
<BarChart
    data={student_attendance}
    x=student_name
    y=attendance_rate
    labels=true
    yFmt=pct0
    color="#3498db"
    height=400
    title="Student Attendance Rates"
/>
```
