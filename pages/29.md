```sql students_attendance
SELECT 
    student_name, 
    attendance_rate
FROM 
    student_attendance
ORDER BY 
    attendance_rate DESC
```

```jsx
<BarChart
    data={students_attendance}
    x=student_name
    y=attendance_rate
    type=grouped
    labels=true
    height=400
    colorPalette={["#4CAF50"]}
/>
```
